WHEN

May 30 – July 28, 2017 Monday – Friday (excluding holidays) 1:00 pm – 5:00 pm (with an occasional, weekly lunch event from 12-1 pm)

WHERE

Dixie State University Smith Computer Center, room 113 St. George, Utah

COST

Free -- selected participants will have tuition covered through scholarships.

ELIGIBILITY

Students entering their junior or senior year of high school, college students, and adults of all ages. Basic programming and web experience is required (CS 1400 and WEB 1400 courses, or equivalent experience). Please note, Code School participants must be accepted. Applicants will be notified of acceptance on or before May 1, 2017.

CURRICULUM

Code School is an intensive nine-week course for students who are interested in jumpstarting their web programming careers. Interaction with the local industry is facilitated by Code School, and many participants are placed in internships and jobs within the software and web industries upon completing the course. Participants will learn modern web technologies, including advanced HTML5 and CSS, JavaScript, databases, frameworks, and client-side and server-side web application development, applied to real-world projects in a concentrated, quick-paced format. No DSU credit will be earned for attending Code School, but the knowledge you will gain can make it easier to complete or test-out of related CIT courses. Most importantly, the connections you will make and the experiences you will gain will be invaluable.
